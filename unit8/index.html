About Me

Hello! I'm Charlotte, a 22-year-old Connecticut native who much prefers Vermont. In the past 12 months, I graduated Summa cum laude from Middlebury College, moved to Washington, D.C., and landed a job at a SaaS company called Opower. In my liberal arts days, I became an Environmental Studies guru (think: Silent Spring, Pigouvian taxes, NEPA, ecosystem services, Love Canal, the whooping crane, Kyoto Protocol, etc.), mastered the edge-case scenarios for the Spanish subjunctive, and tutored peers in writing and editing. In the summers, I pursued my passion for langauge through working in journalism and communications. As a senior, I carried out independent research on the potential for offshore wind energy development on the the Atlantic Coast, which sparked my professional interest in the energy sector. The opportunity for growth, innovation, and leadership in the clean technology and energy efficiency spaces is what motivates me todat.
